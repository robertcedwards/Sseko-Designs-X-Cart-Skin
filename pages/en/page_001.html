The base of every Sseko Sandal is made the same.  However, there are lots of different ways to tie every sandal. 

Want some ideas? Visit www.youtube.com/ssekodesigns.

Have some ideas? Send me a picture or video (to liz@ssekodesigns.com) of your favorite way to wear your kicks and we will post it online! 

Now, with every sandal, the straps are interchangeable. That means you buy one pair of soles and an extra strap or nine and bam! You've got a sandal for every occasion. 

We have some colors that we consistently carry but all the patterns are made in limited quantity. When it is gone, it is gone forever. So if you see one you like…act fast!
